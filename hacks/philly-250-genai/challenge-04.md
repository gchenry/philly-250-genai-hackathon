# Challenge 4: The Assembly

## Introduction
Stitch your generated clips into a cohesive narrative.

## Description
Bring your video assets into **Google Vids**. In this workspace, you will assemble your scenes on the timeline. Focus on the "Director's Cut"—managing transitions and ensuring the pacing is tight enough to fit within a professional 30-to-60 second ad spot.

## Success Criteria
- All scenes assembled in a logical sequence.
- Smooth transitions between historical and modern contexts.
- Total video duration between 30 and 60 seconds.
