# Challenge 1: From History to Narrative

## Introduction
Establish your vision by bridging the gap between 1776 and 2026.

## Description
Use **Gemini** to script a 3-scene narrative that places a Founding Father in a modern Philadelphia context. 

## Success Criteria
- A completed 3-scene script.
- Narrative alignment with Visit Philly brand guidelines.
- Selection of a primary historical protagonist.
