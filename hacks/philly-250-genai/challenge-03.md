# Challenge 3: From Stills to Motion

## Introduction
Bring your storyboards to life using state-of-the-art video generation.

## Description
This is where the magic happens. Use the **Veo Pro model**'s "Reference to Video" capability. Upload your storyboard stills from Challenge 2 and your character contact sheets to generate high-fidelity, 8-second video clips. 

The goal is to ensure that the Founding Father in the video matches the character from your storyboard and maintains visual continuity across different scenes.

## Success Criteria
- Three generated video clips (approx. 8 seconds each).
- Visual consistency maintained between the protagonist in the clips and the storyboard.
- High-fidelity output without significant artifacts.
