# Challenge 5: Audio & Final Polish

## Introduction
Give your commercial a voice and a soul with AI-generated audio.

## Description
The final layer of your production involves voiceovers and music. 
1. Use **Gemini TTS** to generate a compelling tagline or character dialogue.
2. Use **Lyria** to compose an original soundtrack. Aim for a "Modern-Colonial Fusion" that reflects the spirit of 1776 meeting 2026.
3. Layer these elements in Google Vids and export your final masterpiece.

## Success Criteria
- Integrated custom AI voiceover (TTS).
- Original AI-generated soundtrack that matches the mood.
- Final export of the commercial ready for presentation.
